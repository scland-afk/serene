# SEO Audit Quick Reference Checklist

## Critical Issues Found

### ✅ SEO Cannibalization
- [ ] **3 pages competing for "final grading Edmonton"**
  - `/services/final-grading/` (main service page)
  - `/final-grading/st-albert/` (city-specific page)
  - `/blog/final-grading-edmonton/` (blog post)
  
  **Action:** Redefine primary keywords for each page

### ✅ Keyword Stuffing
- [ ] **"Edmonton" appears 22 times** on `/services/final-grading.html`
- [ ] **"final grading" appears 18 times** on same page
- [ ] **"in Edmonton" pattern repeated 8+ times**

  **Action:** Reduce keyword density to 1-2%, use synonyms

### ✅ Internal Linking
- [ ] **10+ internal links** on some service pages (too many)
- [ ] **Generic anchor text** in some places
- [ ] **Missing strategic links** from blog to service pages

  **Action:** Reduce to 5-7 most important links per page, improve anchor text

## Quick Fixes (Can Do Today)

1. **Keyword Reduction**
   - Remove unnecessary city mentions
   - Replace with synonyms naturally
   - Cut "in Edmonton" where location is already clear

2. **Internal Linking**
   - Identify 5 most important links per page
   - Improve anchor text to be keyword-rich
   - Remove or move less important links to footer

3. **Content Differentiation**
   - Service pages = conversion focus
   - Blog posts = educational focus
   - Location pages = local search focus
