About Serene Landscaping

Serene Landscaping is a family-owned landscaping company serving Edmonton and surrounding Alberta communities since 2014. We provide professional landscape design, lawn maintenance, hardscaping, and snow removal services for residential and commercial properties throughout Greater Edmonton.

What started as a small lawn care operation has grown into a full-service landscaping company trusted by hundreds of Edmonton-area homeowners and businesses. We handle everything from simple spring cleanups to complete landscape transformations, but our approach hasn't changed—we treat every property like it's our own and stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee.

We're licensed, insured, and WCB covered, so you can trust that your property and our team are protected. Our crew has over 10 years of combined experience working with Alberta's unique climate, soil conditions, and seasonal challenges. We know what works in Edmonton—and what doesn't.

Our reputation is built on quality work, fair pricing, and honest communication. We show up when we say we will, we do the job right the first time, and we're here to answer your questions before, during, and after the project. Steve Owner, Serene Landscaping

Our Services

What Services Does Serene Landscaping Offer?

We provide complete landscaping services for residential and commercial properties throughout Edmonton, St. Albert, Spruce Grove, and Sherwood Park:

Landscape Design & Installation

Custom landscape design, plant selection, sod installation, garden beds, drainage solutions, and complete front or backyard transformations. We handle projects from initial concept through final installation.

Hardscaping & Patios

Interlocking paver patios, walkways, retaining walls, fire pits, and outdoor living spaces. We work with quality materials designed to withstand Edmonton's freeze-thaw cycles and last for decades.

Lawn Maintenance

Weekly and bi-weekly lawn mowing, edging, spring and fall cleanups, mulch installation, and seasonal property maintenance. Available for both residential homeowners and commercial properties.

Final Grading

Required for new construction properties to meet building code and obtain occupancy permits. We prepare sites for sod or landscaping, correct drainage issues, and ensure proper grading away from foundations.

Commercial Snow Removal

24/7 winter snow clearing for businesses, property managers, and condo boards. Parking lot plowing, walkway clearing, and entrance maintenance to keep your property safe and accessible.

Commercial Property Maintenance

Year-round grounds maintenance programs for office buildings, retail centers, multi-family properties, and property management companies. Scheduled service, consistent quality, transparent billing.

Not sure which service you need? Contact us for a free consultation and we'll walk your property to discuss options and provide recommendations based on your goals and budget.

Questions About Serene Landscaping?

How long has Serene Landscaping been in business?

We've been serving Edmonton, St. Albert, Spruce Grove, and Sherwood Park since 2014—over 10 years of experience working with residential and commercial properties throughout Greater Edmonton.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We're fully licensed, insured, and WCB covered. This protects both your property and our team members. We're a legitimate landscaping business, not a cash-only side operation.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Edmonton, St. Albert, Spruce Grove, Sherwood Park, Fort Saskatchewan, Leduc, Beaumont, Stony Plain, and surrounding Alberta communities within approximately 30 kilometers of Edmonton. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, just call us at (587) 566-9879 and we'll confirm.

Do you work with both homeowners and commercial properties?

Yes. Homeowners hire us for landscape design, lawn care, patios, and seasonal cleanups. Commercial clients—including property managers, condo boards, and business owners—hire us for grounds maintenance, snow removal, and property upkeep programs.

How do you handle pricing and quotes?

We provide free, no-obligation quotes after visiting your property to assess the scope of work. Our written quotes clearly detail what's included, what materials will be used, and what the timeline looks like. No hidden fees, no surprise charges—just honest, transparent pricing.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

We complete a final walkthrough together before considering any project complete. If something isn't right, we address it immediately at no additional cost. We're not satisfied until you're satisfied—that's our guarantee.

Do you offer maintenance after installation?

Yes. Many of our landscape design clients transition to ongoing lawn maintenance programs after their project is complete. After all, who better to maintain your landscaping than the team that installed it? We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and seasonal maintenance options.

For spring and summer landscape projects, booking 3-4 weeks in advance helps secure your preferred dates. Lawn maintenance can often be scheduled within 1-2 weeks depending on our current workload. Snow removal contracts are typically arranged in October or November before winter arrives.
