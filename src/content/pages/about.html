About Serene Landscaping

Serene Landscaping is a family-owned landscaping company serving Edmonton, St. Albert, Spruce Grove, Sherwood Park, Stony Plain, and surrounding Alberta communities. We provide professional landscape design, lawn maintenance, hardscaping, and snow removal services for residential and commercial properties throughout Greater Edmonton.

What started as a small lawn care operation has grown into a full-service landscaping company trusted by hundreds of Edmonton-area homeowners and businesses. We handle everything from simple spring cleanups to complete landscape projects, but our approach hasn't changed - we treat every property like it's our own and stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee.

We're licensed, insured, and WCB covered, so you can trust that your property and our team are protected. Our crew has over 8 years of combined experience working with Alberta's climate and seasonal challenges. We know what works in Edmonton - and what doesn't.

Our reputation is built on quality work, fair pricing, and honest communication. We show up when we say we will, we do the job right the first time, and we're here to answer your questions before, during, and after the project. Ryan S. Owner, Serene Landscaping

Our Services

What Services Does Serene Landscaping Offer?

We provide complete landscaping services for residential and commercial properties throughout Edmonton, St. Albert, Spruce Grove, and Sherwood Park:

Landscape Design & Installation

Custom landscape design, plant selection, sod installation, garden beds, drainage solutions, and complete front or backyard projects. We handle projects from initial concept through final installation.

Hardscaping & Patios

Interlocking paver patios, walkways, retaining walls, fire pits, and outdoor living spaces. We work with quality materials designed to withstand Edmonton's freeze-thaw cycles and last for decades.

Lawn Maintenance

Weekly and bi-weekly lawn mowing, edging, spring and fall cleanups, mulch installation, and seasonal property maintenance. Available for both residential homeowners and commercial properties.

Final Grading

Required for new construction properties to meet building code and obtain occupancy permits. We prepare sites for sod or landscaping, correct drainage issues, and ensure proper grading away from foundations.

Commercial Snow Removal

24/7 winter snow clearing for businesses, property managers, and condo boards. Parking lot plowing, walkway clearing, and entrance maintenance to keep your property safe and accessible.

Commercial Property Maintenance

Year-round grounds maintenance programs for office buildings, retail centers, multi-family properties, and property management companies. Scheduled service, consistent quality, transparent billing.

Not sure which service you need? Contact us for a free consultation and we'll walk your property to discuss options and provide recommendations based on your goals and budget.
