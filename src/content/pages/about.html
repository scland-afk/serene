About Serene Landscaping

Serene Landscaping is a family-owned landscaping company serving Edmonton, St. Albert, Spruce Grove, Sherwood Park, Stony Plain, and surrounding Alberta communities. We provide professional landscaping services for residential and commercial properties across Greater Edmonton.

Our services include landscape design, lawn maintenance, retaining walls, artificial grass, fence installation, and reliable snow removal during the winter season. Each service is planned and installed with long-term performance in mind.

What began as a small lawn care operation has grown into a full-service landscaping company trusted by hundreds of Edmonton-area homeowners and businesses. From seasonal cleanups to complete landscape projects, we approach every job with the same level of care and stand behind our work with a clear satisfaction guarantee.

We are licensed, insured, and WCB covered, so your property and our crew are protected at every stage of the project. With over eight years of combined experience working in Alberta’s climate, we understand local soil conditions, drainage requirements, and seasonal challenges. We know what works in Edmonton and what does not.

Our Services

What Services Does Serene Landscaping Offer?

We provide landscaping services for residential and commercial properties throughout Edmonton, St. Albert, Spruce Grove, Sherwood Park, Stony Plain, and nearby communities.

Landscape Design & Installation

Layout planning, plant selection, garden beds, sod installation, and full yard builds. We design with drainage and long-term maintenance in mind, then install with clean edging and finished grading.

Retaining Walls

Paver patios, walkways, retaining walls, and outdoor living features. We focus on proper base depth, compaction, edging, and slope so your hardscape stays stable through Edmonton weather.

Lawn Maintenance

Weekly and bi-weekly lawn mowing, edging, spring and fall cleanups, mulch installation, and seasonal property maintenance. Available for both residential homeowners and commercial properties.

Final Grading

Final grade preparation for new builds and drainage corrections. We shape and level for proper water flow away from foundations and prep the site for sod or landscape installation.

Artificial Grass

Professional synthetic turf installation with proper base prep and drainage. Low maintenance and durable for backyards, pet areas, and high-use zones.

Fence Installation

Cedar, vinyl, and pressure-treated fences built straight and strong. Posts set 3 feet deep in concrete for stability through Edmonton winters.

Snow Removal

Residential and commercial snow clearing. Driveways, walkways, and parking lots cleared on schedule. Seasonal plans and 24/7 response for commercial.

Commercial Property Maintenance

Year-round programs for commercial properties including scheduled landscaping, seasonal cleanups, and dependable site presentation. Billing stays clear and service stays consistent.

Not sure which service fits your property? Contact us for a consultation and we will walk the site, discuss goals, and recommend a plan that matches your priorities and budget.
