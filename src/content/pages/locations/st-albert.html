Final Grading in St. Albert

In St. Albert, rough grading is reviewed through a Lot Grading Certificate, but the City does not require final lot grade certificates or final grade inspections after rough grade approval.

After rough grade is approved, the homeowner is responsible for ongoing grading maintenance. Topsoil should be applied uniformly, with a maximum depth of 6 inches, to maintain approved drainage patterns and property-line elevations.

Under Surface Drainage Bylaw 14/2015, homeowners must maintain positive drainage away from foundations toward an approved drainage facility. Discharge terminations are to be set back at least 15 cm from adjacent property lines and at least 2 m from the back of public sidewalk.
