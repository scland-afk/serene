Sod Installation Services

We install rolled turf for new lawns and full yard replacements. Whether you're finishing a new build after final grading, redoing a tired lawn, or adding grass where there wasn't any, we handle the base, the rollout, and the aftercare so the lawn takes hold and lasts.

Rolled sod gives you green coverage right away instead of waiting weeks for seed. The trade-off is cost and timing: sod needs the right base and a clear watering plan. We focus on both so the lawn doesn't just look good at first—it roots in and holds up.

The difference between a lawn that sticks and one that browns or bumps is the base. We check drainage and grade, prep soil to the right depth, and lay sod with tight seams and a layout that sheds water. You get a written watering schedule for the first few weeks so roots establish without guesswork.

We offer this service across the Greater Edmonton region. For local timing, pricing, and project photos, see our sod installation page for Edmonton. For ongoing lawn care or full landscape design, we do that too.

What We Do

Scope of work

  • Site prep and topsoil (depth and condition for roots)
  • Grading and drainage check before rollout
  • Removal of old turf or debris when needed
  • Tilling, leveling, and soil amendments as required
  • Rollout with tight seams and staggered joints
  • Cuts around beds, walkways, and hardscape
  • Rolling for soil contact
  • Cleanup and written watering instructions
Our Process

Our Sod Installation Process

  • Site visit and grading check

    We walk the property, check soil and drainage, and confirm the surface is ready for turf. If grading isn't done yet, we coordinate so the base is right before we roll sod.

    01

  • Base prep

    Old grass or debris comes out if needed. We till, level, and fix grade so water runs off. Roots need depth and drainage; we set that up before any sod goes down.

    02

  • Rollout and finishing

    Fresh sod goes down with tight seams and staggered joints. We cut around beds and hardscape, then roll for contact so pieces don't shift. Layout follows drainage so water doesn't pool.

    03

  • Aftercare

    You get a written watering schedule for the first few weeks. Follow it and the lawn roots in; skip it and you risk patchy or dead spots. We leave you with clear instructions, not guesswork.

    04

Who it's for

New builds. Once final grading is done and approved (where the City requires it), turf is the usual next step. We line up with your grading schedule so the site is ready when we roll sod.

Yard redo. Thin, weedy, or beat-up lawn gets stripped, prepped, and replaced with fresh sod. One project, full reset—no more fighting patchy grass.

Grading and sod together. On new construction we can do grading and turf in one go when that works for you. One crew, one timeline, base done right before any grass goes down.

Why work with us

  • Written aftercare. You get a watering schedule for the first few weeks—no guessing.
