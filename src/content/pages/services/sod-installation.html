Our Sod Installation Process

A healthy lawn starts below the surface. We install 4–6 inches of screened topsoil, grade and roll it smooth, then lay a Kentucky bluegrass blend the same day it is cut. Seams are staggered like brickwork and fitted tightly, because gaps dry out and leave visible lines.

  • Site Walk + Plan: We confirm grading, drainage, access, and the right installation approach for your yard.
  • Base Prep: We level and roll the topsoil, correct low and high spots, and cut clean edges along patios and garden beds.
  • Sod Layout: Tight seams, staggered joints, and clean edges help reduce drying and shrinkage.
  • Roll + Water: We roll the sod for full soil contact, then provide a clear watering plan.
