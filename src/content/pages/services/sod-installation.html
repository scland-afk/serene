Our Sod Installation Process

A great lawn starts below the surface. We rake and roll 4-6" of screened topsoil, then lay Kentucky Bluegrass blend sod the same day it's cut. Seams are staggered like brickwork and butted tight—gaps dry out and leave brown lines.

  • Site Walk + Plan: We confirm grading, access, and the right approach for your yard.
  • Base Prep: We rake topsoil smooth, roll it flat, and cut clean edges along patios and beds with a half-moon edger.
  • Sod Layout: Tight seams, staggered joints, and clean edges reduce drying and shrinkage.
  • Roll + Water: We roll sod for contact, then provide a simple watering plan.
