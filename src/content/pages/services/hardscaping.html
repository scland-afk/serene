Quality Materials & Expert Installation

We work with Unilock, Belgard, and Techno-Bloc pavers based on design goals and budget. Bases are excavated 6 to 8 inches, then built with 4 to 6 inches of road crush and topped with 1 to 2 inches of limestone screening, compacted in 2-inch lifts using a plate compactor. Retaining walls use Allan Block or Expocrete systems, with geogrid installed on walls over three feet. Joints are finished with polymeric sand, and edge restraints are staked every 12 inches to keep surfaces stable.
