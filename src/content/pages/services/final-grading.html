Professional Final Grading Services

Final grading is the process of shaping and leveling your yard to ensure proper drainage away from your foundation. It is required for new construction homes, major renovations, and properties with drainage issues. Without proper grading, water pools near foundations, landscaping fails to establish, and long-term damage occurs.

We provide final grading services throughout Greater Edmonton, including Edmonton, St. Albert, Sherwood Park, Spruce Grove, and Stony Plain. Our work includes topsoil placement, slope correction, swale preservation, and preparation for sod or landscaping installation. We work on both new construction and existing properties that need drainage correction.

Final grading is often required before landscape design, sod installation, or hardscaping can begin. Proper site preparation prevents rework and ensures your landscaping investment performs correctly from the start.

Who Needs Final Grading?

Common Final Grading Situations

New Construction Homes: Most municipalities require final grading approval before occupancy or within a set timeframe after possession. New builds in developments like Secord, McConachie, Heritage Valley, Windermere, Erin Ridge, and Jensen Lakes typically need professional grading to meet code requirements.

Drainage Problems: Properties with water pooling near foundations, soggy yards, or basement moisture issues often need grading correction to redirect water flow.

Landscape Preparation: Before sod, patios, or plantings can be installed, the grade must be correct. Poor grading leads to uneven lawns, settling hardscaping, and plant failure.
