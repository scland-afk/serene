Final Grading in Edmonton

Final grading shapes your yard so water drains away from the foundation and the lot is ready for landscaping. We provide final grading services for new construction and drainage correction across Edmonton, St. Albert, Spruce Grove, and Sherwood Park.

Proper grading is the foundation for everything that follows. We shoot grades with a laser level and build 2% slope away from the foundation—that's about a 2" drop over 8 feet. Topsoil goes down 4-6" deep for sod. On clay-heavy lots, we may add drain tile to move water faster.

Final grading is commonly completed before sod installation or a full landscape design build. With over 8 years of local experience, we plan grades that perform well in Edmonton’s conditions.

What a Proper Final Grade Includes

We check elevations at the house, fence line, and lot corners. Soil gets raked smooth and rolled to avoid settling. Transitions to sidewalks sit 1/2" above concrete so sod doesn't get scalped by mowers.
