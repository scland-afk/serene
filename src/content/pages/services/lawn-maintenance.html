What Makes a Good Lawn Maintenance Program

Consistency is everything. A regular schedule prevents overgrowth and reduces stress on the turf. We cut at 3-3.5" in summer—shorter invites weeds and burns out in heat. Blades stay sharp so cuts are clean, not torn. Edges get trimmed every visit.
