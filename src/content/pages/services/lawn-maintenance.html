What Makes a Good Lawn Maintenance Program

Consistency is everything. A regular schedule prevents overgrowth and reduces stress on the turf. We mow at 3-3.5" in summer—shorter invites weeds and burns out in heat. Blades stay sharp so cuts are clean, not torn. Edges get trimmed every visit.

You receive the same standard of care each visit, No changing crews. No uneven results
