How Do Commercial Landscaping Contracts Work?

We offer flexible contract options designed for commercial properties:

Seasonal Contracts (April-October): Weekly or bi-weekly lawn maintenance, spring cleanup, and fall cleanup bundled into one seasonal agreement. Predictable monthly billing makes budgeting easier.

Year-Round Contracts: Combine seasonal grounds maintenance with winter snow removal for complete property care. Single point of contact, consistent service, simplified billing.

Snow Removal Contracts (November-March): Guaranteed winter service with priority response. Contract pricing provides budget certainty compared to per-push pricing during heavy snow years.

Project-Based Work: Landscape installations, parking lot improvements, seasonal plantings, or property renovations quoted on a per-project basis.

All contracts include detailed scope of work, service frequency, pricing breakdown, and clear terms. We review everything together before you commit, and contracts can be customized to your property's specific needs.

How Much Does Commercial Landscaping Cost in Edmonton?

Commercial landscaping costs depend on property size, service frequency, and scope. Here are typical ranges for Edmonton-area commercial properties:

  • Weekly Lawn Maintenance: $150-400+ per visit (small office) to $500-1,500+ (large retail center)
  • Seasonal Maintenance Contract: $3,000-15,000+ for 6-month program (varies widely by property size)
  • Snow Removal Contract: $2,000-10,000+ per season (depends on lot size, frequency triggers, salt/sand)
  • Spring/Fall Cleanup: $500-3,000+ (based on property size and debris volume)
  • Landscape Installation: $10,000-100,000+ (new construction, parking lot landscaping, major renovations)

The most accurate way to get pricing is to request a quote. We'll visit your property, assess your needs, and provide a detailed proposal with clear pricing and service details.

When Should You Book Commercial Landscaping Services?

For Seasonal Lawn Maintenance: Book in February or March to secure your preferred service schedule before spring demand peaks. Most contracts begin in April when grass starts growing.

For Snow Removal Contracts: October or early November, before the first snowfall. This ensures your property is covered when winter weather arrives. Contract slots fill up quickly.

For Landscape Installation Projects: Book 4-8 weeks in advance for spring or summer work. Fall installation works well for certain plants and hardscaping, with less competition for scheduling.

For Emergency or Immediate Service: We can often accommodate urgent needs within 1-2 weeks depending on current workload and season. Contact us as soon as possible if you need quick service.
