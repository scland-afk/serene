Hardscaping Projects Across St. Albert

St. Albert patios often include seating areas, border courses, and lighting sleeves for future upgrades. Walkways connect driveways to front steps, and retaining walls help manage grade changes on sloped lots. We tailor layouts to how you use the space, not just how it looks on paper.

Base Prep and Installation Standards

We excavate 8-10", install 6-8" of 20mm road crush or limestone screening, and compact in 2" lifts. Pavers sit on 1" of bedding sand, then joints are locked with polymeric sand and edge restraints staked every 12".

Retaining Walls, Steps, and Grade Changes

Retaining walls are common in St. Albert yards with split levels or walkout basements. We build on a compacted base, set the first course level, and step back each row for strength. Taller walls use geogrid and proper backfill so they stay straight and drain cleanly.

Steps and landings are built with the same base and block systems so they match the patio or walkway. We set consistent riser heights and use cap stones with clean cuts at corners and returns.

Patios, Walkways, and Borders

Patios and walkways can be built with interlocking pavers, natural stone, or large slab pieces depending on the look and budget. We cut tight joints, set straight lines, and finish with solid edge restraint so the surface holds its shape.

Garden borders and edging help separate lawn from beds and keep mulch in place. We install concrete or stone edging with consistent reveals so the line stays clean after mowing.

If you are planning a pergola, hot tub pad, or outdoor kitchen, we can build the base and leave sleeves for electrical or lighting. That way the hardscape is ready for future upgrades without tearing anything out.

Layout and Drainage Planning

We plan hardscape layouts around how you use the yard: seating zones, grill areas, walk paths, and storage. Slopes are set to move water away from the house and toward existing swales or drains. When grading and hardscaping are done together, the whole yard ties in cleanly without low spots.

Timelines and Pricing

Small patios and walkways can be finished in 2-3 days. Larger patios with walls or steps can take a full week depending on access and cuts. Patio installs typically range from $25 to $45 per sq ft installed. Retaining walls often range from $75 to $120 per sq ft depending on height and block choice.

We confirm layout, material choice, and access before starting so the crew can move efficiently. Quotes include excavation, base prep, and cleanup so there are no surprise add-ons mid project. If you want to match existing pavers or wall block, send photos and we can source close matches. We can also remove old pavers if needed as well.
