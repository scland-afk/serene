Paving Stones in St. Albert

We install paving stones in St. Albert for patios, walkways, and front entry paths. Our crew focuses on straight lines, tight joints, and clean edges so your pavers stay level and look sharp year after year.

We serve homeowners in Braeside, Lacombe Park, Riverside, Erin Ridge, and nearby neighborhoods. Whether you’re finishing a new build or upgrading an older yard, we build patios and walkways that hold up to Alberta’s freeze-thaw cycles.

Paving stones are a durable, low-maintenance alternative to poured concrete. If a section ever shifts or gets damaged, individual stones can be adjusted or replaced without tearing everything out.

Common projects include:

  • Border courses and edge details

Base Prep and Installation Standards

We excavate 8-10", install 6-8" of 20mm road crush or limestone screening, and compact in 2" lifts. Pavers sit on 1" of bedding sand, then joints are locked with polymeric sand and edge restraints staked every 12".
