Final Grading in Edmonton

Edmonton Final Grading Services for New Builds & Renovations

Looking for professional final grading in Edmonton? Serene Landscaping delivers precise rough and finish grading for new construction, renovations, and yard restorations across Edmonton, St. Albert, Sherwood Park, Spruce Grove, and Stony Plain. Proper final grading protects your foundation, prevents water pooling, and meets municipal requirements before sod, landscaping, or hardscaping can begin. Our crew uses laser-guided equipment to establish correct slope and drainage away from your home, ensuring long-term stability and a build-ready surface. Whether you need lot grading after a new build, regrading for drainage correction, or full yard preparation for a landscape project, we handle every step from soil placement to final compaction. Get it done right the first time with Edmonton's trusted grading professionals.
